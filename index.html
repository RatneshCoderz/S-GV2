📖 How to Play

Movement: Use W A S D keys on PC or the virtual joystick on Mobile.

Shooting: The player automatically shoots at the nearest enemy. Make sure to stay close enough!

Survival: Keep an eye on your Health (❤️). Use Bandages and Recovery Potions from the Shop to heal up.

Shop & Upgrades: Use the 🛒 button to access the shop. You can buy new weapons, upgrade them, and purchase defenses like Walls and Auto-Turrets.

The Objective: Survive as long as you can, earn money, kill enemies, and if you are brave enough, buy the Boss Key for $20,000 to challenge the Mega Boss!
